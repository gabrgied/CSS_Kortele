Otter

North American river otter

Lotra canadensis

Semiaquatic mammal belonging to the Weasel family.

Use rocks and empty shells to feed on marine snails, crabs, sea urchins and mussels.

Get attached to certain stones and can retain their favourite ones throughout their lives.

Hold hands while asleep to prevent drifting apart while floating.

Did you know river otters can hold their breath for up to 8 minutes?
